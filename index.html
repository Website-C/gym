Still have questions?

Our team is ready to assist you with membership details, personal training, and more.

Contact Support
What are your opening hours?
We are open 24/7 for Pro and Elite members via biometric access. Staffed hours are from 6 AM to 10 PM daily. Standard members can access the gym between 08:00 AM and 04:00 PM.
Is there parking available?
Yes, we have a dedicated underground parking lot for members located right off Galle Road. Validated parking is available for up to 3 hours per visit.
Do you offer personal training?
Absolutely. We have over 15 certified trainers specializing in bodybuilding, weight loss, functional training, and rehabilitation. You can book a consultation at the front desk or via our app.
Can I freeze my membership?
Yes, memberships can be frozen for up to 3 months for medical reasons or travel, provided you give us 2 weeks notice.
Do you have showers and lockers?
Our changing rooms are equipped with luxury rainfall showers, secure lockers (bring your own lock or rent one), and hair dryers. Elite members get a permanent private locker.
What is the cancellation policy?
We operate on a month-to-month basis for maximum flexibility. Just let us know 30 days in advance if you wish to cancel. No hidden exit fees.
